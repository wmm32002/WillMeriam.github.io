Why I chose UGA?

The University of Georgia has been a part of my life since I was alittle kid. Being raised by two parents who are die hard UGA fans aswell as alumni, sparked my passion for the bulldogs at a very early age. Attending every home football game since I was a child increased my love for the University, hoping that one day I could follow in my parents footsteps and attend UGA. After being accepted into the University, I didn't have to think twice about where I wanted to go. My parents pushed me throughout my high school career to do really good so I could eventually get into UGA and it seemed the workout just fine. I am so glad I decided to come to Georgia and have no regrets so far since I have been here.UGA has always been my home and forever will be.
